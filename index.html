Research Interests

My research interests are remote sensing imagery and computer vision. I am interested in multimodal data fusion that integrates sensing data from different domains. I am also particularly interested in extracting useful information from vast amounts of sensing data and time-series data using machine learning techniques. I am currently working on a task called Cross-View Geo-Localization, which involves matching satellite images with ground images.
Keywords: Remote Sensing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Data Fusion, Image Processing
